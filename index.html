About us

Nitin Kumar & Co Chartered Accountants, situated at Rohini Sector 7, New Delhi, was incorporated in October 2022 by CA Nitin Kumar. We have a multidisciplinary team of accounting, tax and audit professionals with deep sector specific experience across multiple industries.

Our mission is to provide clients with a one stop solution for all their business, financial and regulatory requirements through professional commitment, integrity and quality services.

Audit & Assurance
